About Me

I started my programming journey in 2016. Since then, I have worked on multiple projects in various game, web and app development areas. I have been passionate about programming for many years, always valuing high quality, robust code with a big emphasis on maintainability.

My latest work experience includes developing a VR game at XR Games and a Blazor application for Jaworski Software Solutions. I am the author of three successful Blender add-ons available on Blender Market. Among other projects, I currently work on ModularFPS — an Unreal Engine 5 template for rapid and quality First Person Shooter development.
